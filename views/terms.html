服务条款

What is Dedicated Assistance?
All of our plans include a dedicated virtual assistant (ZA). You'll work with your ZA on a one-to-one basis, continually submitting tasks directly to this person. Your ZA will grow to know you, your work, and your preferences, while learning to anticipate your needs without you having to repeat yourself.


Where will my ZA be located?
All of our ZAs are located in the United States and you'll work with an assistant in the time-zone of your preference.

Do you live outside the U.S.? No worries! We currently have clients in over 60 countries and can happily support your needs—wherever you are. Please be aware that your ZA will be working from their U.S. time-zone when completing your tasks though.


What is the Branded Email feature?
Branded Email is a little addition that makes a huge difference. This feature allows your dedicated ZA to send and receive emails from your domain instead of their @zirtual.com address. This adds a level of professionalism and gives the appearance that your assistant is in-house when desired. Branded email is included in Executive and VIP level plans, while just $99/mo. for Entrepreneur level plans.


Will my ZA be working on the weekend?
Our ZAs work standard business hours, Monday through Friday. You can submit your tasks anytime, day or night, weekday or weekend and your ZA will receive the task(s) the following business day to begin working on them.


Will my ZA be working on holidays?
Zirtual observes major US holidays. Your Zirtual Assistant will be offline and spending quality time with family and friends on these occasions during the 2015 calendar year:

  • New Year's Day (Thursday, January 1st)
  • Day After New Year's Day (Friday, January 2nd)
  • Memorial Day (Monday, May 25th)
  • Day Before Independence Day (Friday, July 3rd)
  • Labor Day (Monday, September 7th)
  • Thanksgiving Day (Thursday, November 26th)
  • Day After Thanksgiving Day (Friday, November 27th)
  • Christmas Eve (Thursday, December 24th)
  • Christmas Day (Friday, December 25th)
  • New Year's Eve (Thursday, December 31st)
  • New Year's Day (Friday, January 1st, 2016)

Can my ZA handle scheduling?
Absolutely. Your ZA can coordinate meetings, schedule appointments, and manage your Google Calendar with ease. Simply share and grant your ZA access to manage your calendar. It's that simple!

ZAs exclusively use and manage Google Calendars. At this time we cannot guarantee support for Outlook, iCal, or other calendar applications, though we will do our best to accommodate your needs.


Can my ZA make purchases on my behalf?
Yes! Your ZA can place orders, make purchases, and schedule payments safely and securely. You can read more about our security measures and policies here.

To share credit card information, passwords, and other sensitive data, simply have your ZA collect your information over the phone. This way, we can ensure your information remains safe. At Zirtual, we are committed to the security of your account!


Can my ZA make outbound phone calls for me?
Sure thing. Your ZA is happy to contact vendors, make reservations, and place phone calls for you. All ZAs are equipped with Google Voice accounts, making calling a cinch.


How about in-bound phone calls?
Yes and no. Every ZA has a dedicated extension available for you to call to submit tasks (or just to chat!). However, this line is available for your convenience. Your ZA cannot accept calls from contacts, vendors, or service providers on your behalf.


Can my ZA keep my inbox organized?
You know it! Delegating your Gmail inbox to your ZA to read, send, and delete messages on your behalf is a great way to stay on top of email and keep your inbox tidy.

ZAs work exclusively out of Gmail and cannot guarantee support for Outlook, Mail, or other mail clients, though we will do our best to accommodate your needs.


Can my ZA edit and format a presentation for me?
They sure can. Your ZA can use Google Presentations to format and edit personal or business presentations. If you require assistance in a professional application like Powerpoint or Keynote, you're welcome to purchase a license for your ZA as long as it's compatible with their operating system and hardware.


Can my ZA work with my project management tools or other specialized software?
All of our ZAs go through a rigorous certification process so that they can best serve you from day one. While we do not offer specific training for specialized software, our ZAs are more than happy to assist if you are able to provide instruction. Please note that we are unable to provide support for any software requiring local desktop download.


Can my ZA help with my accounting needs?
Your ZA will happily find you an excellent bookkeeper or CPA. However, due to certain legalities and a specific knowledge of the domain, ZAs are unable to provide assistance with Quickbooks or other accounting tools.


Can my ZA do design work for me?
Your ZA can certainly research and vet qualified freelancers or graphic design agencies. However, as talented as your ZA is, they cannot personally provide assistance with design projects.


How about video or audio editing?
Much like design work, audio and video editing requires domain expertise which your ZA will not be able to provide. If you're in a pinch to find a good editor though, your ZA will gladly hunt you down the best!

隐私条款

How long does the hiring process take?
The automated task portion of our hiring starts after you submit your first task and can take up to three days, ultimately depending on how fast you complete the assignments. After you complete the process, the hiring team will then begin to evaluate your work. Because of the number of applications we receive, we are unable to give a definitive timeframe for the evaluation portion.

If we do move forward with your application, you will be sent to your first, virtual, face-to-face interview.


Will I hear back after sending in my application?
Because of the high volume of applications we receive, it is difficult for our team to respond to every application that comes through. Rest assured, if your application is a match for what we're looking for, we will absolutely be in touch.


My state is not listed. Will you be hiring in any other states in the future?
We do not have a specific timeframe for opening hiring in other states. Please feel free to check the website periodically to see if our needs have changed.


If I do not make it through the process, will you tell me exactly why I was not moved forward?
Due to the amount of applications our department receives, we are unable to provide specifics to each candidate. If you're interested in applying again in 30 days, we encourage you to read over and become familiar with our job description.


You usually respond quickly to my emails, but it's been over 24 hours and I still haven't received anything! What's going on?
Never fear! Sometimes we get large influxes of candidates which slows our response time. If it has been more than five days and you still have not heard back, please follow up with us by sending an email to hiring@zirtual.com.


Can I re-apply if I am not moved forward?
Yes, you can definitely re-apply! We just ask that you wait 30 days before doing so.


I feel that I am the ideal candidate with tons of experience, but I was not moved forward. Why?
Experience is absolutely valued, but we also take many other factors into consideration. Things like attention to detail, communication style, and confidence are very important to us!
